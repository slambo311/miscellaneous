    My opinion on TF2 and it's community for some reason

    TF2's community shaped the game into what it is today, and without all of their content, who knows what would become of TF2. Hell, after years of no major TF2 updates, the community revived TF2 from the dead, bringing us some of the best updates, both for people making custom content for TF2, and regular players.
